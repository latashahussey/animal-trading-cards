Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is the most popular dog breed in America.

  • Scientific Name: Canis familiaris
  • Average Weight: 64-79 (males); 55-71 (females)
  • Average Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Most Common Name: "Buddy" (male); "Molly" (female)

Labrador retrievers are natural swimmers thanks to their webbed paws, otter-like tail, and water-resistant coat. Even though they are called Labradors, they are actually from Newfoundland.
